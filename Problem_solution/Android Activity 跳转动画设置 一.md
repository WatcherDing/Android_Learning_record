#Android Activity 跳转动画设置 

###创建动画文件

- slide_left_in.xml

```xml
<?xml version="1.0" encoding="utf-8"?>
<set xmlns:android="http://schemas.android.com/apk/res/android" >

    <translate
        android:duration="200"
        android:fromXDelta="-100.0%p"
        android:toXDelta="0.0" />

</set>
```
